How to be an Author

Equipment and Benefits

As an author, you'll need a few things in terms of equipment to get yourself out there into the public eye.

Authors will typically write their books on a computer. The type of computer isn't really a huge deal, but anything that can run something simple like Scrivener, Ulysees, or even Google Docs will work. Having a place to write on the go like a laptop or tablet is a good idea too.

Having spell-check software such as Grammarly also wouldn't hurt. The more presentable your work is, the better the publishers will like it!

A printer also helps out just in general to get a physical copy of any of your work, particularly for your portfolio if you are looking to impress a publisher.
